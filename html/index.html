About

Riley High School will be putting on its first ever make-a-thon, similar to a hack-a-thon. The goal of this event is the give teens and community stakeholders the opportunity to come together and use their skills in the field of computer science to solve problems. This make-a-thon will take place December 7th, 2019 lasting from 9:00am until 3:00pm at Riley High School.
